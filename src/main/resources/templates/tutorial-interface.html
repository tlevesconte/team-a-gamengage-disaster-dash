DisasterDash

A climate change/resilience game.

How to play

(It's a two player game)

At the start of the game, each player has to answer a series of questions based on the climate change disaster chosen and each player has to take a path to the finish line. If they answer a question correctly, they move forward to the next square. If they get the answer wrong, they are presented with a hint for the correct answer. If they still give the wrong answer, then they do not get to move to the next square, they stay in their position, and it is the turn of the second player to go... The first to the finish line wins the game.

*When there is a winner (first to reach the "finish" line) there is a debrief session. Approximate playing time: 30 minutes.
